About Peerwire

Peerwire is a platform about organizing your project and helping the right people find them. It is mainly targeted at software projects, but may be used in any way possible. Let's play through an example:

Steve and Bob want to make a mobile game inspired by Tetris, which they want to call "Megablox". But making a game is too much for to do on their own, so they need to find other people interested in joining.

TL,DR;

You can use an infinite amount of subprojects divide and conquer your project, isolating single tasks and attract specialists for the ones you need help with. This way, you generate what we call the project tree, which allows you to drill down into more and more specific areas of your project.
